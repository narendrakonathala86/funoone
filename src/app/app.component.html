How we help

Our mission

To enable companionship for remote workers by inspiring meaningful connections through fun team building activities.

Our goal

To reduce loneliness for work from home employees as a direct result of self isolation

Value proposition

Provide a healthy alternative to social media to foster real human engagement through Team building

Key Features

Fun-O-One adds a layer to Micorsoft teams with following features promoting team building while turning Virtual 1-1's more fun

Random Pairing

Team members gets paired to 3 random employees within the organization each month to engage

Interactive Bot

Foo aka Fun-O-One bot acts as a virtual employee orchestrating fun activities & team building

Fun activities

With more than 250 fun activities employees have ample ideas to schedule fun team meetings

Breakout Sessions

Breakout sessions which occur every 30 minutes with over 300 Icebreaker topics

3D avatars

Have more fun in break out session with 3D avatars with AI based Camera filter

100% Secure

As a verified app from Microsoft App store, app is easy to onboard and safe to use
